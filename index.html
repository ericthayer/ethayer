About Me

I have over 10 years of design experience working in video production and web development. I started out in San Diego as a motion artist and video editor but quickly learned how to write HTML and CSS. Since moving to Denver, I have worked with small and large companies and served as consultant to others such as HDNet, Page 1 Solutions, and ConneXion Spot.
